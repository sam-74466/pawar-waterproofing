Epoxy Grouting

Epoxy grouting is used to restore structural integrity and prevent leakage in concrete or masonry surfaces.

5-Step Process

  • Inspection & diagnosis
  • Surface preparation
  • Mixing & injection of epoxy
  • Curing & finishing
  • Final inspection

Materials Used

  • High-grade Epoxy resin
  • Injectable grout materials
  • Primer and bonding agents
  • Sealants for finishing

FAQs

Q: Is epoxy grouting permanent?
A: When applied correctly, it can last for many years, maintaining strong adhesion and sealing.
Q: How long does it take?
A: Usually 1–3 days depending on site size and surface condition.
Q: Do you provide warranty?
A: Yes, we offer warranties on both materials and workmanship.
Q: Is it suitable for old buildings?
A: Yes, epoxy grouting is ideal for repairing old or damaged concrete structures.
Q: Cost estimate?
A: Price depends on damage level, accessibility, and surface area. Free inspection available.
