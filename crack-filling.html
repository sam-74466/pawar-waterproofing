Crack Filling

Our crack filling service restores wall and structural integrity by sealing gaps and preventing moisture ingress, ensuring long-term durability.

5-Step Process

  • Inspection & crack mapping
  • Surface cleaning and preparation
  • Filling with epoxy or PU compounds
  • Finishing and curing
  • Final quality inspection

Materials Used

  • Polyurethane and epoxy resins
  • Polymer-based crack sealants
  • Cementitious fillers
