Our Story & History

Pawar Waterproofing began as a small family-run unit focused on fixing leaks and quickly grew into a trusted service provider for residential and commercial clients. We combine field experience with modern materials and careful diagnostics so you get a long-lasting solution, not a temporary fix.

2016 — Founded

Started offering terrace and small-scale basement repairs in Pune.

2018 — PU & Epoxy Systems

Added PU injection and epoxy grouting services to handle active leaks and structural repairs.

2021 — Expanded to PMC/PCMC

Served larger society and commercial podium projects across Pune and Pimpri-Chinchwad.

2023 — Mumbai & Indore Coverage

Started servicing projects in Mumbai & Navi Mumbai, including residential complexes and small industrial sites.
