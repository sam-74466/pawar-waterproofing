Commercial Shade Waterproofing

We provide coating and membrane systems for industrial, commercial, and residential sheds that protect against water leaks, UV damage, and heat buildup.

5-Step Process

  • Inspection & diagnosis
  • Surface preparation & crack repair
  • Application of coating materials
  • Curing and protection layer
  • Testing & final handover

Materials Used

  • Polyurethane and epoxy coatings
  • Elastomeric membranes
  • Primers, sealants, and top coats
