PU Injection Waterproofing

Polyurethane injection is a fast, permanent solution for sealing cracks, stopping active water leaks, and restoring structural integrity in concrete. Ideal for basements, roofs, and retaining walls.

Importance 5-Step Process Materials

5-Step Process

  • Inspection & diagnosis
  • Prepare & clean substrate
  • Inject PU foam under pressure
  • Curing and protection
  • Testing & handover

Materials Used

  • Polyurethane / Epoxy resins
  • Primers & surface sealants
  • PU foam injection machines
