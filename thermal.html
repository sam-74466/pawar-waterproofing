Why Thermal Scanning Matters

Thermal imaging is an advanced diagnostic technique used to detect temperature variations across surfaces. For waterproofing, it helps identify hidden leaks, dampness, and insulation defects that aren’t visible to the naked eye.

How It Works

  • Infrared Cameras: Capture temperature differences across walls, ceilings, and flooring.
  • Moisture Mapping: Pinpoints exact locations of seepage without damaging surfaces.
  • Analysis: Our team interprets heat signatures to identify leakage sources.
  • Reporting: You get a detailed visual report highlighting affected zones.
  • Solution Planning: We recommend targeted repair and waterproofing solutions.

Benefits

  • Detects hidden water leakages and pipe failures.
  • Non-destructive testing – no drilling or breaking required.
  • Reduces repair time and cost by identifying the problem precisely.
  • Also useful for electrical hotspot detection and energy audits.

Applications

Our thermal scanning service is ideal for:

  • Residential flats and villas
  • Commercial complexes and hotels
  • Industrial plants and factories
  • Basements, terraces, tanks, and walls

We use top-grade thermal cameras (like Hikmicro and Bocsh) to ensure pinpoint accuracy and professional reporting for your peace of mind.
